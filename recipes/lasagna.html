Lasagna

Description

This is how a well cooked Lasagna looks like

Next you'll find the ingredients list and the required steps for making the lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Take the pasta and place in a pot.
  2. Add the cheese and make layers with the pasta.
  3. Pour the sauce over the layers.
  4. Lastly, put the pasta into the oven for 30 mins.
